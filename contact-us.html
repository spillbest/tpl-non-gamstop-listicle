What You Can Contact Us About

We read everything that arrives. The messages that help this site most fall into a few categories:

  • Questions about a review — anything unclear about a rating, a bonus calculation or a withdrawal-time figure we published.
  • Outdated information — if Casino Alpha has changed its wagering requirement or Casino Beta its withdrawal cap and our table still shows the old value, tell us; corrections are processed first.
  • Casino suggestions — a licensed operator you think we should test. Include the licence number if you have it; unlicensed brands are not reviewed.
  • Partnership and business enquiries — affiliate programme managers and operators. Note that commercial agreements never influence scores or ranking positions.
