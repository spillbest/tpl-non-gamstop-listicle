About Us — Who We Are and How We Review Casinos

Last updated: 12 June 2026 Independent editorial team · informational content only

How We Review Casinos

This site exists to answer one question honestly: which licensed casinos publish fair terms and keep them? Every operator we cover goes through the same documented process before a single word is published — we register a real account, read the complete terms, deposit, test support and request a withdrawal. The five criteria below carry every score we give.

ranking-methodologyAbstract evaluation illustration · 1024×1024
Criterion 1

Licensing

Valid licence verified on the regulator’s public register, published owner, fund segregation.

Criterion 2

Games

Provider breadth, live tables, published RTP and lobby filters that actually work.

Criterion 3

Bonus terms

Wagering, weighting, caps and expiry quoted verbatim and checked with worked examples.

Criterion 4

Payment testing

Real deposits and withdrawals timed on a verified account, not copied from marketing pages.

Criterion 5

Mobile

Lobby, search and limit tools tested on a small viewport over a mobile connection.

Scores are re-checked whenever an operator changes its published terms; the last-updated date on each page tells you when that happened most recently.

Editorial Independence

We are an affiliate site: when you visit an operator through one of our links and register, we may receive a commission. That is how this site is financed, and we state it plainly rather than in a footnote.

What commissions never do: they never change a score, a ranking position or a verdict. The methodology above is applied identically to every brand, and operators cannot pay to be listed, to move up, or to have a weakness removed from a review.
  • Rankings are produced by the editorial team only — the commercial side has no veto.
  • Weaknesses (withdrawal caps, high wagering, missing support channels) are published even for top-rated brands.
  • If an operator’s real conditions worsen, the review is updated and the score lowered — several brands have been delisted this way.

Content on this site is informational. We describe operators’ published conditions; we do not encourage anyone to gamble.
