Editorial Standards and Testing Process

No review is published from screenshots or press releases. The standard for every operator on this site:

  1. Register a real account under the operator’s standard sign-up flow.
  2. Deposit real money with at least one mainstream payment method.
  3. Read the complete bonus and payment terms; quote requirements verbatim in the review.
  4. Complete identity verification and time how long KYC actually takes.
  5. Request and receive a withdrawal before publishing — payout claims are tested, never assumed.
  6. Re-verify the published terms whenever an operator changes them, and refresh the last-updated date.
Independence: commissions from affiliate links finance the site but never reach the scoring. Methodology weights are fixed in advance and applied identically to every brand — see the about page for the full disclosure.
